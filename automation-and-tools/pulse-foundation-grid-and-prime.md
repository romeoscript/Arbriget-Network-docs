# PULSE Design: Multi-Tiered Operational Diversity

Arbridge's PULSE ecosystem is delivered through three operational tiers, each tuned for a distinct capital profile and participation style. All three run on the same underlying PULSE engine.

## Pulse Foundation

The foundational layer of the PULSE system — a stable-growth automation engine engineered for consistency and resilience across highly dynamic market conditions.

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Target daily return | ~0.8% |
| Operating cadence | Continuous, 7 days a week |
| Minimum entry | **$100** |

Targeting approximately 0.8% daily returns, Foundation executes trades with precision and minimizes exposure to volatility. Its low entry point makes it an accessible gateway for disciplined, long-term growth — ideal for members seeking steady, predictable yield generation while benefiting from automated intelligence.

## Pulse Grid

The intermediate tier of the PULSE ecosystem, designed for higher-frequency trading cycles and more extensive market penetration.

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Target daily return | Up to 1.2% |
| Minimum entry | **$1,000** (with flexibility for increased allocation) |

Grid's architecture leverages sophisticated AI strategies to optimize execution across multiple decentralized exchanges (DEXs), enabling enhanced arbitrage opportunities, cross-chain swaps, and dynamic liquidity utilization.

## Pulse Prime

The flagship tier of the PULSE ecosystem — a highly dynamic and adaptive automation model engineered to maximize high-frequency trading efficiency.

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Target daily return | 1.6% |
| Minimum entry | **$10,000** |
| Seed capital maturation | 90 days |
| Early-withdrawal fee | 30% |

Pulse Prime employs a combination of predictive AI, probabilistic validation models, and real-time market telemetry to navigate volatile market conditions and exploit arbitrage opportunities across multiple blockchain networks.

Seed capital matures over 90 days, and early withdrawals incur a 30% adjustment fee to safeguard system integrity, maintain liquidity balance, and ensure sustained strategy performance.

## Live transparency across all tiers

All PULSE engines are seamlessly integrated within the Arbridge automation infrastructure, allowing members to monitor trades executed by AI agents in real time. Through the **Live PULSE API**, users gain direct visibility into transactions conducted on major DEXs such as **PancakeSwap** and **Uniswap**. Every executed trade, strategy adjustment, and market signal is instantly reflected in the interface — providing a verifiable audit trail, operational transparency, and actionable insight.
