# The Arbridge Membership

The Arbridge Membership is your verification pass into the Arbridge environment. It is not a subscription to a static product — it is a license to interact with a living digital infrastructure that evolves alongside the markets it observes.

## What membership unlocks

- Full access to **Pulse**, the proprietary AI-powered execution engine
- Entry to the **Arbridge Horizon** and **Arbridge Vertex** automation tiers
- Real-time analytics through the Arbridge Dashboard
- Continuous access to the **Arbridge Education Hub**
- Annual execution-vault updates, new strategy modules, and future platform integrations

## Membership model

Membership runs for a twelve-month cycle. During this period, members receive uninterrupted access to every tool, module, and upgrade released within the cycle. New capabilities ship continuously — members are not asked to re-purchase features that become part of the standard offering.

## Verification

Onboarding includes identity verification consistent with the [AML Compliance Policy](../learning-and-resources/aml-compliance-policy.md). This is an essential safeguard that keeps the network clean, compliant, and aligned with institutional standards.

## What membership is not

Membership does not grant equity, it does not represent a security, and it does not guarantee any specific return. It is an operational license — a durable connection between a verified participant and the Arbridge execution fabric.
