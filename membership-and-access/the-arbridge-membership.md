# Arbridge Membership

A subscription to the Arbridge Network provides full access to its integrated ecosystem. Once activated, users gain unlimited entry to **PULSE** — a system featuring Smart Assistants, a comprehensive educational hub, advanced analytics capabilities, and probabilistic validation models.

## Term and scope

Each subscription is valid for a **three-month term**, ensuring uninterrupted access to all Arbridge functionalities, ongoing PULSE enhancements, and future system integrations.

More than a standard subscription, this membership functions as a **license** — enabling users to engage with an evolving digital infrastructure designed to adapt and scale in real time.

## What membership unlocks

- Unlimited access to **PULSE** and its Smart Assistants
- The Arbridge **Education Hub**
- Advanced analytics and probabilistic validation models
- Continuous PULSE enhancements and system integrations delivered within the term
- Direct interaction with the [Arbridge Dashboard](the-arbridge-dashboard.md)
